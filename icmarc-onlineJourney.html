Introducing Start Your Journey, ICMA-RC’s new online enrollment experience.

Welcome to a more secure, streamlined approach to enrollment. Start Your Journey focuses on a paperless, multi-touchpoint journey designed to ease your administrative burden, while improving the enrollment experience for your employees.


Need help or have questions? Discuss going digital with us.

Talk to an expert

Once you’ve transitioned to online enrollment, take advantage of the many benefits, including:


More convenient and efficient plan administration

Secure submission of employee personal information

Up-to-date investment information regarding your plan’s fund lineup

Accessible reporting of enrollment data


Help your employees start their savings journey.

As part of ICMA-RC’s forward-thinking approach, our traditional enrollment kits will transition into the Start Your Journey paperless enrollment experience after December 11, 2020.
